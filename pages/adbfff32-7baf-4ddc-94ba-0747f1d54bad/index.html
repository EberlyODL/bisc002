Upon completion of this lesson you should be able to:

  1. Describe the activities of Darwin that got him thinking about evolution.
  2. Give a list of observations Darwin made that would aid his development of the theory of evolution.
  3. Explain how natural selection is the mechanism through which evolution works.
  4. Compare and contrast Darwin's and Lamarck's mechanisms for evolution.
  5. Describe the ideas of others that Darwin used to develop his theory.
  6. Compare and contrast the methods and conclusions reached by Darwin and Wallace.
