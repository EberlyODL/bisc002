Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this lesson you should be able to:

  1. Describe how early peoples attempted to explain the natural world
  2. Give a list of contributions of each historical figure to the development of scientific thought
  3. Explain the nature of science.
  4. Compare and contrast the core terms used by scientists.
  5. Describe the limits of science.
  6. Describe the contributions of early thinkers of evolution
  7. Compare and contrast the view of nature before and after Darwin's ideas of evolution.
