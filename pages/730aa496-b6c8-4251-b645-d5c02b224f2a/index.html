The earth has seen 10 separate extinction events. The top 5 occurred at the following times in earth history:

  • Near the end of the Ordovician period (443 mya)
  • Near the end of the Devonian period (360 mya)
  • At the end of the Permian period (250 mya)
  • At the end of the Triassic period (210 mya)
  • At the end of the Cretaceous period (65 mya)
