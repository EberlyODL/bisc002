AZT is a drug once used to treat AIDS. The idea was to cause as many mutations as possible in the HIV pathogen to hopefully kill it. Unfortunately AZT also gets incorporated into the DNA of healthy cells and kills them too. The drug has pretty toxic effects and now much better drugs are available to treat AIDS.
