Learning Objectives

Upon completion of this lesson students will be able to:

  • Describe the structure and role of a chromosome
  • Describe a gene
  • Describe what homologous chromosomes are
  • Explain the difference between Haploid and Diploid

This lesson reintroduces students to the following terms:

  • Chromosome
  • Homologous Chromosome
  • Genome
  • Gene
  • Allele

Following this lesson, students will be able to define each of the common terms with in the context of basic Mendelian Genetics.
